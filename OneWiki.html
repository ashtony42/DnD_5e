Starting Professions
Every hero in the world starts their journey in some job type of profession which form the foundation of how they develop.

Farmer- Built strong to do large macro physical activities.
(Build +2, Dex +1, Int +0)
Spear lvl 1 (weapon skill)
Horse Riding lvl 1 (ability to compose one's self while on a horse)
~5 starting skill points~ 200 copper

Merchant- Built weak, but have inter-personal skills and are generally more knowledgeable.
(Build +0, Dex +0, Int +3)
Learning lvl 1 (+1 ability point per level of skill per level)
Persuasion lvl 1 (chance to sway another's thoughts to what you want)
~6 starting skill points~ 500 copper

Dock Worker- Built average, but focus on their skills.
(Build +1, Dex +2, Int +0)
Shipwright lvl 1 (ability to build, repair and modify ships)
Sailing lvl 1 (ability to operate a boat)
~5 starting skill points~ 200 copper

Cook- Built average, bus focus of preparing food and recovery of fatigue.
(Build +1, Dex +1, Int +1)
Cooking lvl 1 (ability to produce food that keeps people healthy)
Treat Wound lvl 1 (ability to help cure wounds faster through natural remedies)
~5 starting skill points~ 200 copp
